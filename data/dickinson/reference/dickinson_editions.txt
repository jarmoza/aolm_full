Bianchi collections

1) Bianchi, Martha Dickinson, ed. (1924), The Complete Poems of Emily Dickinson, Boston, MA: Little, Brown, and Company
Includes poems from volumes of 1890, 1891, 1896, and 1914, with some rearrangement and 3 additional poems.

2) Bianchi, Martha Dickinson; Hampson, Alfred Leete, eds. (1930), The Poems of Emily Dickinson (Centenary ed.), Boston, MA: Little, Brown, and Company
Includes contents of 1924 collection, plus the 1929 volume.

3) Bianchi, Martha Dickinson; Hampson, Alfred Leete, eds. (1937), The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Boston, MA: Little, Brown, and Company
Includes contents of 1930 collection, plus the 1935 volume.
